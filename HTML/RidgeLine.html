Here we can see an estimation of the distribution of temperatures (minimum and maximum) using a cosine kernel density stimator (decided to used the cosine since is less sensitive to ouliers and is very suitable). The differences between years are almost unnoticeable, this is due the scale of the graph and that the changes in each year are (in magnitude) very small.

Even that the visualization is quite simple and doesn't have interaction, the preprocessing of the data is quite intensive due to the initial shape of the data retrieved.
