Section 3: Distributions and Timelines

Line Chart Radar Chart Ridgeline

For the line chart I decided to add a region dropdown list so it's possible to see how the temperatures vary in different regions of the country.

Three principal groups of lines/circles can be seen. The bottom lines are for the minimum temperatures, while the top lines are for the maximum. The circles represent the average temperatures.
The color palette is given by a year, that way it is possible to identify the bottom and top lines of the same year, as well as the average points.

You can hover over any line/circle to see evolution of the minimum, maximum and average temperatures of that year independently. Also, you can click on the rectangles of the legend to compare different years, which is very useful, since this way we can see that the 2022 year is (generally) hotter than the others in almost every point.
